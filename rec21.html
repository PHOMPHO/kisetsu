Kisetsv (/hisetsʊ/) is the spirit of the seasons, being able to control the temperature and weather depending on their mood he lives mostly hidden in the Shenkuu mountains which houses the shrines and temples dedicated to him and his siblings, the spirit of the wind and the spirit of the sea.

According to the legends, all three of them where born in the beginning of time itself, the colorful wind gained the shape of a dragon bound by the air, the sparkling waters gained the form of a dragon bound by the waters, and the flowerbeds gained the form of a dragon bound by the earth. The people believe these three deities are the guardians of each plane of existence, the divine plane where the Gods rest; the astral plane where the dead reside; and the physical plane where the humans dwell.

Kisetsv is a calm and gentle creature that keeps themselves mostly outside of the human gaze, living mostly in solitude inside the dense forests that surround Shenkuu. Protective of the nature and the other creatures in the forest he tries to maintain the balance between the seasons to help the growth of those that live in the physical plane.

However, they can be moody from time to time as well, making the weather and, consequently, the seasons around them change even if they don't necessarily mean to cause such havoc. Things seen as 'small' or 'insignificant' by humans are generally what causes such changes, like the destruction of nature or not praying respect o the deities protecting them from disasters.

To those closer to him like their siblings and the other deities in the Shenkuu pantheon, Kisetsv is seem as a bit childish and impulsive in his decisions to help or abdicate from acting on something regarding the humans. After all, between the other deities they are the youngest among them all.

When happy, he tends to skip around the forest and bloom flowers that may not even be in season, to the human world this is seen as a gift from the deities, a signal that they are happy with their offers. On the other hand, when stressed or sad he tends to sulk inside his temple and consequently the temperatures dawns and light snow may cover the temple itself, acting almost like a protective barrier to keep others outside.

Neopia is a planet in the Goldilocks Zone of an unknown star system, at an orbital distance to produce years of 365.25 days (equivalent to Earth). It has a day/night cycle that is 24 hours in length. It supports a wide array of life forms, many species of which collectively are grouped together as Neopets.❞ ー The Book of Ages

Home to many different kinds of creatures, including Neopets and Faeries, Neopia is quite abundant with resources. This world is divided into two main continents. Once a calm and ordinary world it has seen it's fair share of wars and red baths. Shenkuu however is an imperial city surrounded by mountains, forest and mist. This is the heart of the Shenkuu Empire, and is the main port for the Cyodrake's Gaze whenever it returns from trading missions. The buildings here are perched atop the mountain peaks, and bound together by rope bridges that stretch through the mists. The Emperor makes his home here, in the Palace. The city is the only currently publicly known land in Shenkuu, but as it is an Empire, there are likely to be other settlements elsewhere. Shenkuu is rather inhospitable. It is located in the mountainous region west of Neopia Central, and north of Altador. There are a number of fast flowing rivers that snake down the mountains, and grassy areas have grown up by their edges. Locations are often named by landmarks, such as Mountain-in-the-Mist. The mists almost permanently hang around the peaks of the mountains, obscuring the valley floors. Above the mist layer, the weather is normally cool and crisp, typical of normal mountain air. The orchid species Selerium Taniverens grows in small amounts in Shenkuu.

Other then himself, there are quite a few other deities worshiped by the pantheons of Shenkuu as protectors of all we can and cannot see. Above the spirits there are 6 gods that created the material and astral plane: the Goddess of the Sun, the God of the Stars, the God of the Storms, the Goddess of the Moon, the Goddess of Dawn and the God of Night. These six entities then created the different spirits to protect and watch over the realms, in total there are 5 spirits: the spirit of the sea, the spirit of the wind, the spirit of the season, the spirit of thunder and the spirit of fire.

Taiyo (太陽): Goddess of the Sun and creator of light, worshiped as the birthing mother of the universe and it's planes.
Hoshi (星): God of the Stars and creator of space and the planets, worshiped as the god of growth and life.
Arashi (嵐): God of the Storms and creator of balance and caos, worshiped as the god of justice and war.
Tsuki (月): Goddess of the Moon and creator of time, worshipped as the god of passage and soothing.
Yoake (夜明け): Goddess of Dawn and creator of the days, worshiped and a god of luck and fortune.
Yoru (よる): God of Night and creator of the astral realm, worshiped as the god of death and rebirth.

Umi (海): spirit of the sea and protector of the astral plane.
Kaze (風): spirit of the wind and protector of the divine plane.
Kisetsu (季節): spirit of the seasons and protector of the physical plane.
Kaminari (雷): spirit of the thunder and protector of the mental plane.
Hi (火): spirit of the fire and protector of the rivers of life.

As mentioned before, these spirits are worshiped as protectors and called upon by the people for help and guidance. Generally these praying either at their own altars at home or at the temples scattered throughout the city. The 3 major temples are the Forest Temple, dedicated to Kisetsu where people go to ask for help with harvest or health, the Wind Temple dedicated to Kaze where people go to ask for help with guidance and protection and the Temple of Water dedicated to Umi where people fo to ask for help with travels and to connect with the dead.

The other two temples, the Temple of Thunder and the Temple of Fire are located inside the city itself instead of scattered throughout the region. The Temple of Thunder sits inside the palace at it's entrance to guide those that need assistance with their mental health, while the Temple of Fire sits inside the Watch Tower close to the Cyodrake's Gaze to guide the new living into a path of light.

As for the higher deities, the gods, they can only be communicated with by the priestesses that live and study inside the Lunar Temple. These priestesses dedicate their lives to specific gods and generally only ask for help in times of dire need, since it wouldn't be well taken by the spirits if they where ignored as the first line of help. The population in general do worship the gods with gifts and altars, but they do not receive direct messages in the same way the priestesses do.


As Taiyo was born from the dust of the cosmos as a being of light an grace she felt nothing but lonliness, so se searched for anyone around her. Tsuki was the first one she found, the lonely girl sat atop a round shape that emited a very soft light. As the two became aquainted, the third God appeared, Hoshi, bathed in sparkling lights he gave Taiyo the idea of creating a small planet where they could care and watchover other creatures as they went about. Arashi, however, intervined, "If there is not balance there can never be life!", and so Yoake and her brother Yoru joined in as well, "If there is not a cicle there can't be balance.", with that all six of them decided to create different ways of balance that together would form a perfect system.

And so, Taiyo created the Sun and the earth those creatures would live in, but separeted them into four planes, the divine place where her and the others would watch them from, the physical plane where the living creatures would live and the mental plane where the thoughts and ideas of the creatures would be. Hoshi on the other hand, created planets all around the first one so that all of them could spin and see the beautiful light created by Taiyo.

Soon after, Yoake created the day, which would be bathed by the Sun and Yoru created the night bathed Moon, he also created the astral plane, where the creatures that passed on could reflect and continue their missions to enlightment. Consequently, Hoshi created the beings that would live in this planet, both flora and fauna, and Arashi gave them different stregths and preferences so the strongest and most resourceful would trive.

With this, however, the young Gods noticed they wouldn't be able to care for everything alone, and so they decided to create the spirits. Umi was created by Yoru to protect the bodies of water and it's creatures but also protect the border between the astral and physical planes. Kaze was created by Tsuki to protect the winds and the border between the divine and the astral planes. Kisetsu was created by Hoshi to protect the forests and it's creatures but also the border between the physical and astral planes. Kaminari was created by Arashi to create rain and thunder but also to protect the border between the mental and physical plane. Lastly, Taiyo created Hi, which would protect the rivers of life which created all the creatures that permeated the planes.

Subsequently, the spirits drifted away into this new world, bathing it in color and warmth.
